Reaper

Reaper is a wraith-like terrorist who sets out to kill his former comrades to feed his desire of revenge.

 

His Hellfire Shotguns, the ghostly ability to become immune to damage, and the power to step between shadows make Reaper one of the deadliest beings on Earth. Beside these abilities, Reaper is able to jump, crouch, reload and perform Quick Melee.
