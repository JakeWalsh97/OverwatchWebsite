McCree

Gunslinger and vigilante, McCree makes use of his revolver to slay his foes, flashbangs to stun, and has a combat roll to avoid danger.

 

McCree is a powerful anti-flanker and medium range damage dealer. His damage falls off a cliff at longer ranges, and his low mobility means that he has to take conventional routes to get up close to his opponents, making it easy to outflank him and kill him before he has a chance to fire back.

 

  • - Weapon: Peacekeeper
  • - Ultimate Ability: Deadeye
  • - Difficulty Rating:
    ☆☆
